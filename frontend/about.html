Our Story

Born out of love for authentic Indian flavour

Curry Time was founded in 2008 in the heart of Faridabad with a single purpose: to bring the warmth, richness, and depth of genuine Indian cooking to every guest’s table — without compromise.

We know the importance of a strong first impression. It’s why we invest in every detail — from how our doors look to how our hosts welcome you with a warm smile. The fabric of Curry Time was born out of love and respect for authentic recipes, met with a desire to bring quality ingredients to the table.

Simply put, we’re here to bring you an experience you can feel great about — every single visit.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Six principles that guide everything we do — from sourcing ingredients to serving guests.

01

Quality First

We never compromise on the quality of ingredients. Every item on our menu is made with the finest produce sourced daily.

02

Authentic Flavours

Our recipes carry generations of culinary wisdom. We preserve traditional techniques while presenting them in a modern way.

03

Guest Experience

Every guest deserves to feel welcomed and valued. Our team is trained to deliver warm, attentive service every visit.

04

Community

We’re proud to be a part of Faridabad’s dining scene. We support local suppliers and give back to our community.

05

Transparency

We believe in honest pricing and clear communication — no hidden charges, no surprises. Just good food at fair prices.

06

Continuous Improvement

We actively seek feedback and constantly refine our menu, service, and ambiance to exceed your expectations.
